Dilations

To dilate a figure, we make it smaller or larger from a fixed point.

In our case, we will dilate all our figures from the origin. This means that we can determine the new coordinates of our figures by multiplying by a scale factor, which is the ratio of the lengths of two corresponding sides of two similar polygons.
