Example 2

if triangle ABC is congruent to triancle DEF, angle A is 20 degrees, and angle B is 80 degreees. What is the measure of angle F?

All triangles are 180 degrees, so:

∠A + ∠B + ∠C = 180 degrees
20 + 80 + ∠C = 180 degrees
∠C = 80 degrees

Thus, since the two trianges are congruent, and angle C is corresponding to angle F, the measuer of angle F must also be 80 degrees.
