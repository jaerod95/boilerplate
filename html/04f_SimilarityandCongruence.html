Similarity and Congruence

Now that we have learned about similarity and congruence, we can look at a figure and determine if it is similar, congruent, or neither.

  • Figures that are congruent will have the same angle measures and the same side lengths.
  • Figures that are similar will have the same angle measures, but the side lengths will be proportional to each other.
  • Figures that are neither similar nor congruent will either have different angle measures or the side lengths will not be proportional to each other.
