Unit 2

Lines, Angles, and Polygons

Shapes in our world are created when lines cross each other at different angles. When a line crosses parallel lines, then the angles that are created are equal to each other.

In this unit we will examine parallel lines that are cut by transversals, and determine which angles are congruent. We will also examine the angle measures inside and outside of different shapes.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Find the relationship between angles created by lines and transversals.
  2. Find missing angle measures from figures with parallel lines and transversals.
  3. Find angle measures inside and outside a triangle.
  4. Find the angle measures of interior and exterior angles of polygons.
