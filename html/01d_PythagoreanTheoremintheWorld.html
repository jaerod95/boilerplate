Pythagorean Theorem in the World

There are many different situations in the world in which we can use the Pythagorean theorem to find a distance, length, or value that we do not know. When we use the Pythagorean theorem in these situations, we identify which sides of the triangle are the legs and which side is the hypotenuse. Remember that the hypotenuse is the longest side.

Construction is an area where they use the Pythagorean theorem regularly. When you are building a home, you want the walls to be at right angles to the floor. The construction workers use the Pythagorean theorem in their measuring to make sure that the walls and the floor create a right angle.

To help with these problems, you should draw a picture. As you create a picture, you have a model of where the hypotenuse and the legs are, and what values to place in each of those areas.
